Section 3.2: My analysis and explanation of the data¶

From the data, we can observe that there is the same general trend in all 3 of these statistics. The number of points, assists and rebounds seem to have gone down during between 2000-2010 and increased again from 2010-2023 with the last 2 seasons having the highest number in all 3 statistics. I predict that this trend will continue and the total points scored will continue to increase as time goes on. I make this prediction that this is happening since the game is continuing to change every decade. Last decade, the players played a slower-paced and more defense oriented version of basketball meanwhile this decade, with the huge influence that 3 pointers have had on basketball, the game has become more dynamic and fast-paced than ever before .

Section 4.1: Is the first to get drafted the best?¶

Section 4.3: Finding the regression line¶

The next step was calculating the slope using the formula:

$$\text{slope} = r * \frac{SD(y)}{SD(x)}$$

Where $r$ is the correlation coefficient between $X$ and $y$, and $SD$ stands for standard deviation.

In [ ]:
slope = df_relevant.corr()['pts']['draft_number']*(df_relevant['pts'].std()/df_relevant['draft_number'].std())
slope
Out[ ]:

Then I calculated the y-intercept of the regression line using the formula as follows:

$$\text{intercept} = \text{avg}(y) - \text{slope} * \text{avg}(X)$$
In [ ]:
intercept = df_relevant['pts'].mean() - slope *df_relevant['draft_number'].mean()
